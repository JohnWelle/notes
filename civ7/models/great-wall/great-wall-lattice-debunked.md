# Yield Findings

## Major Discovery

Adjacency is weaker than originally modeled.

Original mental model:

```text
GW adjacency
    ↓
feeds more adjacency
    ↓
runaway scaling
```

Actual model appears closer to:

```text
Tile yield

+
GW base yield

+
GW adjacency yield
```

without recursive stacking.
