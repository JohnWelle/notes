# Terms

## Scope

**This document is intentionally incomplete.**

It is not intended to document every mechanic or concept. New entries are added only after repeated observation shows that their absence materially degrades collaborative reasoning, causes repeated misunderstanding, or increases reconstruction effort.

Until then, explain the concept in the current session rather than expanding the documentation. The documentation should evolve from recurring evidence, not anticipated need.

## Game Terms

Terms that are used in-game, e.g. terms that are used in the game UI to categorize different types of objects/game mechanics.

### gold

`gold` is used as a term interchangeably about _either_ gold as a **resource** or gold as a **yield**.

## Other Terms

Terms that are commonly used to explain higher order concepts that are not used in the game but commonly used in the community or by ourselves in internal documentation.

### meta

`meta` could refer to [meta.md](meta.md) file where we add clarifications and guidelines when it comes to collaborative methodology as we trod along, or..

It is commonly used in the community as a term to define specific strategies or synergies, typically emerging from larger updates and patches of the game that affects game mechanics, e.g. the rural/urban meta shift discussed [here](../2026-06-30-tecumseh-tonga/CHARTER.md).
