## Core Rules / Assumptions

- Great Walls can be built on improved non-resource tiles, including mines.
- No forest chop production bonus in 1.4.0.
- Forest/jungle tiles are unusually valuable post-1.4.0.
- Antiquity adjacency does not carry into Exploration.
- Han walls retain base culture but lose adjacency.
- Ming GW is mandatory; entire run is built around maximizing it.

## Strategic Principles

1. Expansion first.
2. Preserve future GW corridors whenever practical.
3. Avoid unnecessary buildings that consume premium GW tiles.
4. Growth matters because ring expansion unlocks future GW tiles.
5. Tempo matters more than theoretical perfection.
